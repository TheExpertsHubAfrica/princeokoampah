About me

Prince Okoampah is a powerful voice in Africa’s digital and leadership space—an award-winning public speaker, tech entrepreneur and versatile branding strategist who is passionate about equipping the next generation of leaders in Africa.

Recently honored with the Global Excellence Business Leader Award by the HonorCrest Institute (Canada), Porto Business School (Portugal) and M-Gibes College of Business & Management (UK & Ghana), Prince is recognized for his outstanding leadership and impact across the continent at a live event in Johannesburg, South Africa.

He is a Harvard-trained communication expert, with an MBA in Leadership for Multilateral Organizations from Atlanta College of Liberal Arts and Sciences (Georgia, USA). Prince Okoampah’s blend of innovation, psychology and strategy helps CEOs under 40 build powerful brands, lead with clarity and integrate AI for smarter, faster results.

Through platforms like The EmpowerED Talks and Trail Blazers Africa, Prince inspires thousands with his message of purpose-driven leadership, growth and Trail-Blazing impact. He has delivered keynotes, trained TEDx speakers and coached C-suite leaders across various industries.

Named Ghana’s Under-30 Tech CEO of the Year, Prince’s approach to leadership is grounded in authenticity, empowerment and evolution. He believes that true leadership is about rising and lifting others along the way.
